## FAQ

### 1. Is StackSpend free to use?
Yes. The audit is completely free and requires no login. Email is optional for receiving the full report.

---

### 2. How does StackSpend calculate savings?
We compare your current AI tool usage (plans, seats, API spend) against optimized configurations and pricing benchmarks.

---

### 3. Do you access my accounts or billing data?
No. All inputs are manually provided. We do not connect to any external accounts or billing systems.

---

### 4. How accurate are the results?
Estimates are based on real pricing data and common usage patterns across engineering teams. They are directional but actionable.

---

### 5. Who is this tool for?
It’s built for engineers, founders, and teams using multiple AI tools who want visibility into hidden subscription and API waste.
