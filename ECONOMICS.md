# ECONOMICS MODEL — STACKSPEND (CREDX CONTEXT)

This document estimates the unit economics of StackSpend as a lead-generation product for Credex.

All numbers are directional assumptions based on early-stage B2B SaaS benchmarks.

---

## 1. Value of a converted lead

Credex monetizes through AI infrastructure credits and discounted SaaS provisioning.

Assumptions:
- Average customer savings identified: $200–$800/month
- Credex capture rate (commission / margin / spread): ~20–30% of savings
- Average deal duration: 6–12 months

### Conservative estimate:
- $300/month savings × 24 months = $7,200 total savings
- Credex capture (25%) ≈ $1,800 gross value per lead

👉 **Estimated value per qualified lead: $1,000–$2,000**

---

## 2. CAC estimates by channel (GTM-based)

Since StackSpend is organic-first, CAC is primarily time + distribution effort.

### Reddit (r/startups, r/SaaS)
- CAC: ~$2–$10 equivalent (time + posting effort)
- High variability, low scale reliability

### Hacker News (“Show HN”)
- CAC: ~$0–$20 equivalent
- High upside, unpredictable traffic spikes

### Discord communities (DevTools, AI builders)
- CAC: ~$5–$15
- Requires manual engagement

### Direct outreach (LinkedIn / email founders)
- CAC: ~$10–$30 per qualified audit
- Highest conversion rate channel

### X (Twitter) organic posting
- CAC: ~$0–$5 (if post performs well)
- Strong compounding effect, but inconsistent

---

## 3. Funnel conversion assumptions

### Step 1: Audit completed → lead capture
- 25–40% conversion (email gate after value shown)

### Step 2: Lead → Credex consultation booked
- 10–20% conversion
(only high-savings users convert)

### Step 3: Consultation → purchase of credits
- 20–40% conversion

---

### End-to-end conversion:
Audit → purchase ≈ 0.5% to 3%

This is consistent with high-intent B2B lead funnels.

---

## 4. Revenue per 1,000 audits

Assume:
- 1,000 audits completed
- 30% email capture = 300 leads
- 15% consultation booking = 45 calls
- 30% conversion = ~13 customers

Revenue:
- 13 customers × $1,500 avg value = **$19,500**

👉 Effective revenue per 1,000 audits:
**~$15K–$20K range**

---

## 5. Path to $1M ARR in 18 months

To reach $1M ARR:

### Target:
- $83,000 MRR equivalent

Assume:
- $1,500 value per customer over lifecycle
- Need ~660 active paying equivalent customers/year

### Required scale:
- ~50,000–70,000 audits/month
- OR strong virality loop (shareable audit results)

### Required conditions:
1. Strong SEO + viral sharing of audit URLs
2. High-savings detection rate (>30% of users show meaningful savings)
3. Credex conversion pipeline operational (fast follow-up)
4. Consistent presence in devtool communities
5. Low friction audit completion (<2 minutes input)

---

## 6. Key economic insight

This product is not optimized for ARPU directly.

It is a **lead qualifier for high-intent savings discovery**.

The economics only work if:
- audits feel “surprisingly accurate”
- savings feel real and actionable
- Credex conversion is tightly integrated into high-value cases

Without that loop, CAC > LTV. With it, this becomes a high-margin inbound funnel.
