# StackSpend — AI Spend Audit Tool

StackSpend is a free AI spend audit tool designed for startup founders and engineering teams using multiple AI products like Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, and API-based models.

The app analyzes AI tool subscriptions, identifies overspending opportunities, and generates actionable recommendations with estimated monthly and annual savings.

---

## Live URL

`https://stackspend-mauve.vercel.app/`

---

## Who this is for

- Startup founders
- Engineering managers
- Small SaaS teams
- AI-heavy product teams trying to reduce infrastructure/tooling spend

---

## Features

- AI tool spend input form
- Per-tool optimization recommendations
- Monthly + annual savings calculations
- AI-generated personalized audit summary
- Shareable public audit reports
- Lead capture + backend storage
- Open Graph / Twitter preview support
